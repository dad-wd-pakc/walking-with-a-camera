David Delmonte - Walking With A Camera

David specialises in the unusual: photographing artworks in a legal 'graffiti' tunnel. Taking images of 'rubbish' as found, not manipulated.
Shooting washing-lines all over the world. Taking photographs of people taking photographs of other people, and themselves. Liquorice Allsorts
that echo the work of 'abstract' artists, and anything that appeals to him whilst he is out taking his camera for a walk.
All his images are available as prints and the 'graffiti' poster is a limited edition, see CONTACT for details.
